## 📰 Key Takeaways

> OpenAI recently unveiled its public policy agenda covering four core pillars: AI safety, youth protection, workforce transition, and global AI standards. On safety, OpenAI stresses aligning AI development with broader societal interests to prevent uncontrolled risks. Youth protection focuses on safeguarding minor users from improper AI system impacts through both product design and regulatory measures. Workforce transition addresses the structural impact of widespread AI adoption on the job market and explores ways to help workers adapt to new tech environments. On global standards, OpenAI calls for governments and industries to collaborate on consistent AI governance frameworks to avoid fragmented regional standards.
