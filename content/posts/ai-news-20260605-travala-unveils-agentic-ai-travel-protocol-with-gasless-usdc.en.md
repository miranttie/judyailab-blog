## 📰 Executive Summary

> Travala is a blockchain-based travel booking platform that recently launched an agentic AI travel protocol on Base, Coinbase's Layer 2 chain. The protocol's key highlights include two features: first, a gasless USDC booking payment mechanism, where users don't need to pay additional gas fees when booking hotels on-chain, lowering the barrier to crypto payments; second, AI-assisted payment execution, where AI agents assist with completing the payment process instead of requiring users to manually handle each on-chain step. The overall design direction integrates autonomous AI agents into travel booking scenarios, making on-chain transactions smoother and more transparent for everyday users.
