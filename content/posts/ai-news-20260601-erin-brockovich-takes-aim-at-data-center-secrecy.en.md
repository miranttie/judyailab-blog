## 💬 JudyAI Lab's Take

Erin Brockovich's shift from environmental pollution cases to data center transparency advocacy sends an unmistakable social pressure signal to the AI industry.

Her past victories came from "making complex pollution data accessible to the public"—that same logic now extends to AI infrastructure. This alone tells you one thing: information transparency is seeping from environmental issues into the tech industry's social responsibility framework.
